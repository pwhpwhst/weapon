Pumas are large, cat-like animals which are found in America.
定语从句，被动语态

When reports came into London Zoo that a wild puma had been spotted forty-five miles south of London,they were not taken seriously. 
时间状语从句，被动语态，过去完成时，过去一般时，名词性从句


However, as the evidence began to accumulate, experts from the Zoo felt obliged to investigate, 
for the descriptions given by people who claimed to have seen the puma were extraordinarily similar.  
时间状语从句，因果关系（for），过去分词，定语从句


The hunt for the puma began in a small village where a woman picking blackberries saw 'a large cat' only five yards away from her.
介词作定语（The hunt for the puma），地点状语从句，现在分词短语作定语，描述远近


It immediately ran away when she saw it, and experts confirmed that a puma will not attack a human being unless it is cornered（adj.被困得走投无路的）. 
时间状语从句，情态动词（will）

The search proved difficult, for the puma was often observed at one place in the morning and at another place twenty miles away in the evening.
被动语态，罕见定语（place twenty miles away）


Wherever it went, it left behind it a trail of dead deer and small animals like rabbits. 
地点状语从句，状语倒装（behind it）

Paw prints were seen in a number of places and puma fur was found clinging to bushes. 
现在分词短语作补足语


Several people complained of 'cat-like noises' at night and a businessman on a fishing trip saw the puma up a tree. 
介词作定语（a businessman on a fishing trip），宾语补足语（up a tree）


The experts were now fully convinced that the animal was a puma, but where had it come from ? 
被动语态，名词性从句，过去完成时


As no pumas had been reported missing from any zoo in the country, 
this one must have been in the possession of a private collector and somehow managed to escape.
原因状语从句，被动语态，过去完成时，现在分词短语作补足语，特殊（must have been,must 后使用完成时 表示对过去事件的推测），特殊（manage）


The hunt went on for several weeks, but the puma was not caught. 

It is disturbing to think that a dangerous wild animal is still at large in the quiet countryside.
名词性从句（it），动词不定式（to think）
