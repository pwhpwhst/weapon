1、在maven的setting中配置：

<repository>
	<id>spring-snapshots</id>
	<name>Spring Snapshots</name>
	<url>https://repo.spring.io/libs-snapshot</url>
	<snapshots>
		<enabled>true</enabled>
	</snapshots>
</repository>



cd C:\Users\Administrator\Desktop\spring_cloud_config
mvn clean package
